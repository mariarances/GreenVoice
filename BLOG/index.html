ABOUT GREENVOICE

What is GreenVoice?

GreenVoice is a blog that raises awareness about environmental issues affecting the world and the Philippines while encouraging people to protect and care for the environment.

GreenVoice aims to inform readers about global warming, climate change, biodiversity loss, and improper waste management. Through articles and discussions, we explain their causes and effects while sharing ways individuals and communities can help protect the planet.

By understanding these environmental challenges, people can become more responsible and take part in protecting the environment for future generations.
